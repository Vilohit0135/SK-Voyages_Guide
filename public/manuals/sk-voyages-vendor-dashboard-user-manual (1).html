Welcome

The Vendor Dashboard is your daily control room for SK Voyages trips. It helps you see today's spending, active and completed trips, supervisors, tickets, cancellation requests, wallet activity, invoices, and profile settings.

i
Good to know Your exact menu may look shorter if your account has limited permissions. That is normal. You only see the areas your vendor admin has allowed.

Basics

Signing in

Use your registered email or phone

The sign-in screen is for opening your secure vendor workspace. Enter the login details given to you by SK Voyages or your vendor admin.

  1. Open the Travellink dashboard link.
  2. Enter your email or mobile number and password.
  3. Tap Sign in.
  4. If you cannot enter, ask your vendor admin to check your status and permissions.
!
If your vendor is inactive Some actions may be blocked until the account is active again. Contact SK Voyages support if this happens.

Basics

Getting around

Use the left menu first

The left sidebar is the fastest way to move between Dashboard, Supervisors, Trips, Reports, Cancellations, Tickets, Account, and Settings.

  • Tap Trips to open Active, Completed, or Scheduled Trips.
  • Tap Reports to switch between Supervisor Report and Company Report.
  • Tap your profile area or Sign out at the bottom when needed.

Core areas

Dashboard

Your daily snapshot

The Dashboard is for a quick health check. It shows Today Spend, Live Hold Amount, Created Trips Today, Remaining Balance or Credit Usage, top supervisors, and recent completed trips.

  • Tap + Add Money if you are prepaid and need more wallet balance.
  • Tap Spend or Trips in the supervisor chart to change the view.
  • Tap a recent trip to open its details.
OK
Daily habit Start here every morning. Low wallet balance or high live hold amount can affect trip handling.

Core areas

Supervisors

Manage supervisor access and activity

The Supervisors screen lists the supervisors under your vendor. Use it to search, filter by status, open a supervisor profile, and review trip and spend activity.

  • Tap a supervisor row to view details.
  • Use filters to find active or inactive supervisors.
  • Check the detail page for today's completed trips and recent bookings.
09
Core areas

Trips

Follow active and completed work

The Trips area is split into Active Trips, Completed Trips, and Scheduled Trips. Active Trips show what is happening now. Completed Trips show finished journeys and fare details.

  1. Tap Trips in the sidebar.
  2. Choose Active Trips for live work or Completed Trips for past journeys.
  3. Use search or filters to find a trip by route, supervisor, driver, date, or status.
  4. Tap a row to open the trip detail modal.
10
Core areas

Scheduled Trips

Watch upcoming bookings move forward

Scheduled Trips are grouped by Pending, Published, and Driver Assigned. This helps you see which future trips still need attention.

  • Tap Pending to see trips not yet ready for a driver.
  • Tap Published to see trips visible for driver assignment.
  • Tap Driver Assigned to confirm the driver is already linked.
11
Core areas

Reports

Review performance and spending

Reports help you compare completed trips, spend, and supervisor or company activity for a selected period.

  • Tap Supervisor Report to review one or more supervisors.
  • Tap Company Report to review company-level activity.
  • Use date filters before exporting or reading totals.
12
Core areas

Cancellations

See cancellation reviews

The Cancellations screen shows Pending Requests and Resolved Requests. Pending items are still under review by the admin team.

  • Tap Pending Requests to see open cancellation reviews.
  • Tap Resolved Requests to see final decisions.
  • Use fault filters such as Driver, Supervisor, Both, or None to narrow the list.
13
Core areas

Tickets

Handle driver report tickets

Tickets are support items raised around completed trips or driver reports. They are grouped into Pending Tickets and Resolved Tickets.

  1. Open Tickets.
  2. Choose Pending Tickets to see what still needs attention.
  3. Tap a ticket to read the trip, driver, notes, and activity history.
  4. Check Resolved Tickets when you need the past decision.
14
Core areas

Account

Watch wallet, ledger, and invoices

The Account area shows current balance, total credited, total debited, ledger filters, spending summaries, and invoicing where available.

  • Tap Credit or Debit to filter ledger entries.
  • Tap Invoicing to view billed, collected, outstanding, pending, overdue, paid, or voided invoices.
  • Open an invoice row to see more details.
15
Core areas

User Management

Add team members carefully

User Management is for vendor admins. It lets you add team members, set role labels, choose permissions, and activate or deactivate access.

  1. Open User Management.
  2. Tap the add member button.
  3. Enter basic info, then choose module permissions.
  4. Save only when the role matches the person's real work.
!
Permission reminder Give the smallest access that lets a person do their job. Account and settings access should stay limited.
16
Core areas

Settings and Profile

Keep company details current

Settings and Profile show vendor details, contact information, document status, PAN card, GST certificate, and password options.

  • Tap Profile Details to review company information and documents.
  • Tap Password to change your password.
  • Check document badges: Approved, Pending, or Not Submitted.

FAQ

Why do I not see all menu items?

Your account may be a team member account with limited permissions. Ask your vendor admin if you need access.

Why is money shown as a hold amount?

Live Hold Amount is money reserved for in-progress trips. It changes as trips complete or cancel.

Which trips appear in reports?

Reports focus on completed trips for the selected period, so pending or cancelled trips may not be included in totals.

Can I change invoice status?

Where allowed, pending or overdue invoices can be updated. Paid or voided invoices are usually not changed from the list.

Glossary

Hold Amount

Money reserved for live or pending trips until the trip is settled.

Supervisor

A company user who creates or manages trips under your vendor.

Ledger

A list of wallet credits and debits.

Completed Trip

A trip that has finished and is counted in reports.

Ticket

A support item connected to a driver report or trip issue.

Credit Usage

Postpaid spending against an approved credit limit.
